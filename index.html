About Hidden Chapters

Blending Emotion, AI, and the Joy of Discovery.

Our Mission

Hidden Chapters began as a simple idea: what if you could choose a book based on a feeling, not just a cover? It has since evolved into a full-stack application that blends the magic of surprise with a powerful recommendation engine.

Our mission is to create a space where technology enhances the joy of reading. We use a content-based recommendation system to offer meaningful suggestions, while the "blind date" concept keeps the experience playful and exciting.

Core Features

  • AI-Powered Recommendations
    Using TF-IDF and cosine similarity, our backend finds books with similar themes and styles.
  • Blind Reveal
    Read a captivating teaser before the title is revealed, creating a moment of pure discovery.
  • AI Story Chain
    Co-create a unique story with fellow readers and Google's Gemini AI.
